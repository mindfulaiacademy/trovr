# Trovr Berlin Launch Plan
### Youth Football Coach Directory — Berlin, Germany

*This document adapts the main GTM strategy for the Berlin launch market.*

---

## MARKET CONTEXT: BERLIN

**Launch Market**: Berlin, Germany (all Bezirke)
**Sport Focus**: Football (Fußball) only at launch
**Language**: English-first (Berlin expat + international community, bilingual German speakers)
**Currency**: EUR
**Coach Price Range**: €50-€75 per Einzeltraining (60-90 minutes)
**Credential Standard**: DFB-Lizenzen (C-Lizenz, B-Lizenz, A-Lizenz, Torwarttrainer, Kindertrainer-Zertifikat)

### Why Berlin

- Founder's home base with existing network of 10+ coaches ready to onboard
- Large international/expat community comfortable with English-language platforms
- Dense youth football ecosystem: 400+ Vereine registered with BFV (Berliner Fußball-Verband)
- High demand for private Einzeltraining beyond Verein — parents actively seek supplementary coaching
- No platform currently serves this niche in Germany

### Berlin-Specific Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Model | Gap for Trovr |
|---|---|---|
| **Vereinssuche (BFV)** | Free club directory | Only clubs, no private coaches, no reviews |
| **Google/Google Maps** | Organic search | No verification, no football-specific context, mixed results |
| **Kleinanzeigen (eBay Kleinanzeigen)** | Free listings | Unverified, no reviews, mixed with everything else |
| **Instagram / WhatsApp** | Word of mouth | Unstructured, no verification, limited reach |
| **Private coach websites** | Self-hosted | No discovery, no reviews ecosystem |

**Key insight**: There is NO dedicated platform for finding verified private football coaches in Germany. Zero direct competitors.

## ADAPTED CHANNEL STRATEGY

### Channel 1: WhatsApp & Telegram Groups (Highest Priority for Berlin)

**Why**: In Berlin, parent communication runs through WhatsApp groups, not Facebook. Every Verein, every Schulklasse, every Bezirk parent network has a WhatsApp group. This is where word-of-mouth happens.

**Tactics**:
- Leverage founder's existing Verein and parent WhatsApp network for initial spread
- Ask onboarded coaches to share their Trovr profile link in their client WhatsApp groups
- Create shareable coach profile links optimised for WhatsApp preview (Open Graph tags)
- Build a "Trovr Berlin Parents" WhatsApp community channel for updates

**Timeline**:
- Week 1-2: Share with personal network, get initial 10 coaches to share profiles
- Week 3-4: Ask satisfied parents to forward to their Verein WhatsApp groups
- Week 5-8: Organic spread across Bezirke as parents recommend coaches

**Budget**: €0
**Target**: 200 parent profile views/week by Month 2

### Channel 3: Facebook & Instagram (Expat Communities)

**Why**: While German parents use WhatsApp, Berlin's large expat community (200K+ internationals) uses Facebook groups actively for recommendations.

**Target Groups**:
- Berlin Expats (100K+ members)
- Berlin Parents (various groups, 5-20K members)
- Berlin International Families
- AFEB (Association of Families with English-speaking Children in Berlin)
- Berlin Kids Activities

**Tactics**:
- Post value content: "How to choose a Fußballtrainer for your child in Berlin"
- Share coach spotlights with Berlin context
- Engage in recommendation threads when parents ask for coach referrals
- Instagram content showing Berlin training locations, coach spotlights

**Budget**: €0-200/month
**Target**: 500 clicks/month from expat groups by Month 3

---

### Channel 4: Verein Partnerships

**Why**: Berlin has 400+ registered football Vereine. Many parents seek extra training beyond the 2x/week Verein sessions. Vereine themselves often get asked "who do you recommend for private training?" and have no good answer.

**Tactics**:
- Partner with 5-10 Vereine in the launch phase
- Offer: "Recommend verified coaches to your families" — Trovr as the official supplementary training directory
- Co-branded "Find a private trainer" page for each partner Verein
- Attend Verein events and tournaments for visibility

**Timeline**:
- Week 1-4: Approach 10 Vereine through founder's network
- Month 2-3: Secure 5 partnerships, create co-branded pages

**Budget**: €0 (relationship-driven)

## LEGAL COMPLIANCE (GERMANY)

- [x] **Privacy Policy** (DSGVO/GDPR compliant) — created at `/site/privacy.html`
- [x] **Impressum** (required by § 5 TMG) — created at `/site/impressum.html`
- [x] **Cookie Consent Banner** — implemented with accept/decline, respects user choice
- [x] **Google Analytics IP Anonymisation** — enabled
- [ ] **Background check process** — adapt for Germany (Führungszeugnis / erweitertes Führungszeugnis for working with children)
- [ ] **Data Processing Agreement (AVV)** — needed for any third-party processors
- [ ] **Update Impressum** with full legal entity details once business is registered

**Note on Führungszeugnis**: In Germany, the equivalent of a background check for working with children is the "erweitertes Führungszeugnis" (extended certificate of good conduct). Coaches should provide this as part of their verification process.
