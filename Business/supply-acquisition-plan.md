# Trovr Supply Acquisition Plan — Coach Onboarding

*Last updated: 2026-03-22*

---

## Why Supply First

In a two-sided marketplace, coaches must come before parents. A parent who opens Trovr and sees 3 coaches leaves and never returns. A parent who sees 25 verified coaches across 8 Bezirke starts browsing. The magic number for Berlin launch: **minimum 15 coaches across 5+ Bezirke before any parent-facing marketing begins.**

This plan gets you there.

---

## Phase 0: Preparation (Days 1–3)

Before reaching out to a single coach, have these ready:

### 0.1 — Coach Pitch Materials

**One-page pitch deck (PDF/WhatsApp-shareable):**
- What Trovr is (1 sentence): "A directory where Berlin parents find verified Fußballtrainer — background-checked, DFB-verified, with real reviews."
- What the coach gets: Verified profile, parent discovery, booking system (coming), video showcase
- What it costs: **Free for the first 25 coaches (Gründerpreis)** — Complete Showcase at €0/month for the first 3 months, then locked at €15/month for life
- ROI math: "Your profile gets seen by parents who would never find you on WhatsApp. One new client = €60/session × 4 sessions/month = €240/month from a €15 investment."
- CTA: "Send me your DFB-Lizenz photo and a 60-second video intro, and I'll build your profile this week."

**Create in:** Canva or Figma. German + English versions. Optimized for WhatsApp forwarding (single image + short text).

### 0.2 — Coach Onboarding Form

Simple Tally or Typeform collecting:
1. Full name
2. Phone / WhatsApp number
3. Email
4. Bezirk(e) where they coach
5. DFB-Lizenz type (C/B/A/Torwarttrainer/Kindertrainer)
6. Upload: Photo of DFB-Lizenz (for verification)
7. Session price (€/hour)
8. Age groups they coach
9. Short bio (3–5 sentences)
10. Profile photo
11. Optional: 60-second video intro (can be filmed on phone)
12. Optional: 2–3 existing client references (for initial reviews)

### 0.3 — Profile Build Capability

You need to be able to turn a completed form into a live profile on Trovr within 24 hours. This means:
- Template for coach profile page is ready
- Photo upload / hosting works
- Video embed works (YouTube unlisted or direct hosting)
- The coach can share their profile link immediately after it's live

### 0.4 — Founder's Rate Terms

Document the Gründerpreis offer clearly:
- First 25 coaches: Complete Showcase tier free for 3 months
- After 3 months: locked at €15/month for life (vs. €25 standard)
- In exchange: coach agrees to share their Trovr profile link with existing clients and provide 2–3 parent testimonials
- This is not a discount — it's a partnership. Early coaches shape the platform.

## Phase 1: Personal Network (Days 4–10)

**Goal: 10 coaches onboarded from Andreas's existing contacts**

You mentioned having 10+ existing coach contacts in Berlin. This is your unfair advantage.

### Step 1.1 — List Your 10+ Contacts

Write down every coach you know personally or through one degree of separation:
- Coaches from your own Verein connections
- Coaches your kids have trained with
- Friends who coach
- Coaches you've seen at Sportplätze
- Coaches recommended by parents you know

### Step 1.2 — Warm Outreach (WhatsApp, 1-on-1)

**Do NOT post in groups yet.** Personal 1:1 messages convert 10× better than group posts.

**Message template (German):**

> Hey [Name], ich baue gerade eine Plattform für private Fußballtrainer in Berlin — Trovr. Eltern können dort verifizierte Trainer finden, mit DFB-Lizenz-Badge, Bewertungen und Video-Profil. Ich suche die ersten 15 Trainer für den Launch und du bist einer der Ersten, an die ich gedacht habe.
>
> Für die ersten 25 Trainer ist alles kostenlos — komplett. Kein Haken. Ich baue dir dein Profil, du schickst mir ein Foto, deine Lizenz und 2-3 Sätze über dich.
>
> Interesse? Ich schick dir das Formular.

**Message template (English, for expat coaches):**

> Hey [Name], I'm building a platform for private football coaches in Berlin — Trovr. Parents find verified coaches with DFB credential badges, reviews, and video profiles. I'm onboarding the first 15 coaches for launch and you were one of the first people I thought of.
>
> For the first 25 coaches, everything is completely free. No catch. I'll build your profile — just send me a photo, your license, and 2-3 sentences about your coaching.
>
> Interested? I'll send the form.

### Step 1.3 — Follow Up

- If no response after 24 hours: one follow-up ("Hey, just checking — did you see my message about Trovr?")
- If interested but slow: offer to do a 10-minute WhatsApp call to fill out the form together
- If hesitant: address the #1 objection ("It's free, you keep 100% of your session fees, and your profile is live within 24 hours. Try it, if you don't like it I'll take it down.")

### Step 1.4 — Build Profiles Same Day

When a coach submits the form:
1. Build their profile on Trovr within 24 hours (ideally same day)
2. Send them the live link via WhatsApp
3. Ask: "How does it look? Anything you'd change?"
4. Then: "Can you share this link with 3–5 of your current clients? Their reviews will show up on your profile."

**Target: 10 coaches with live profiles by Day 10**

## Phase 3: Superprof Poaching (Days 15–25)

**Goal: 10–15 coaches migrated from Superprof to Trovr**

There are ~73 football coaches listed on Superprof Berlin. These are coaches who already understand the value of a platform listing — they just chose the only option available. Trovr is a strictly better option for them.

### Step 3.1 — Build a Target List

Go to [superprof.de/unterrichtsangebot/fussball/berlin](https://www.superprof.de/unterrichtsangebot/fussball/berlin/) and identify:
- Coaches with DFB credentials mentioned in their profile
- Coaches with 3+ reviews (proven quality)
- Coaches charging €35–75/hour (your target price range)
- Coaches with professional-looking photos

Create a spreadsheet: Name, price, reviews, credentials, Bezirk, contact method.

### Step 3.2 — Book a Free First Lesson

Superprof's model: 94% of coaches offer the first session free. Use this to your advantage:
- Book a free session with 10–15 target coaches (you or a friend with a child)
- During or after the session, introduce Trovr in person
- This is the highest-conversion outreach possible — face-to-face, after they've invested time in you

**Alternative (faster, less personal):** Contact coaches through Superprof's messaging system. You'll need a Student Pass (~€29–39) for one month. Cost: €39 to reach 73 coaches. Worth it.

### Step 3.3 — The Pitch to Superprof Coaches

**What they're getting on Superprof:**
- Generic listing among piano teachers and math tutors
- No DFB credential badge
- No video portfolio
- Parents pay €29–39/month just to message them (friction)
- No booking integration

**What they get on Trovr:**
- Football-specific platform — parents searching specifically for Fußballtrainer
- DFB credential verification badge (trust signal)
- Video portfolio and custom intro
- Free for parents to contact them (zero friction)
- Online booking (coming soon)
- Background check badge (trust premium)
- **Free for the first 25 coaches (Gründerpreis)**

**Message template:**

> Hi [Name], ich bin Andreas und baue Trovr — die erste Plattform speziell für private Fußballtrainer in Berlin. Ich habe dein Superprof-Profil gesehen und deine [DFB-Lizenz / Bewertungen / Erfahrung] sind genau das, was Eltern suchen.
>
> Der Unterschied zu Superprof: Trovr ist nur für Fußball, mit DFB-Lizenz-Verifizierung, Video-Profilen, und kostenlos für Eltern (kein Abo nötig um dich zu kontaktieren). Für die ersten 25 Trainer ist es komplett kostenlos.
>
> Hättest du Lust, dabei zu sein?

### Step 3.4 — Handle Objections

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I already have Superprof" | "Keep it! Trovr is additional visibility, not a replacement. But parents on Trovr search specifically for football coaches — on Superprof you compete with 100K tutors." |
| "I don't need more clients" | "A stronger profile helps you raise your rates. Verified coaches with video portfolios charge 20–30% more." |
| "I don't trust new platforms" | "Fair. It's free, I'll build your profile, and if you don't like it in 30 days I'll remove it. Zero risk." |
| "How many parents use it?" | "We're launching with 20+ coaches. Parent marketing starts once we have enough coaches to be useful. You're getting in before the demand wave." |

**Target: 10–15 Superprof coaches contacted, 5–8 converted by Day 25**

### Step 5.3 — The Verein Partnership Value

What Vereine get from working with Trovr:

## The One Rule

**Every coach who joins should bring at least one more coach.** If your existing coaches aren't referring others, something is wrong — either the profile isn't good enough, or the coach doesn't see value yet. Fix that before scaling outreach.

The best supply acquisition channel isn't ads, SEO, or cold outreach. It's a coach who tells another coach: "This thing actually works."
