3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds under Art. 6 GDPR:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): For analytics cookies and marketing communications
  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): To provide the platform service to coaches and parents
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): To improve our service, prevent fraud, and ensure platform security

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To display coach profiles and enable parent discovery

  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interest
